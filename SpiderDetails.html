Welcome to the Spectacular World of Spider-Man!

Spider-Man is a fictional superhero created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko for Marvel Comics. First appearing in 1962, Spider-Man quickly became one of the world's most popular and recognizable superheroes. Peter Parker, an ordinary teenager, gains superpowers after being bitten by a radioactive spider.
