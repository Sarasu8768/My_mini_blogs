ARMY - Pride of the Nation

The Army is the shield of a nation. It defends our borders, protects our freedom, and stands tall in the face of every threat. Soldiers sacrifice their lives so that the country lives in peace and pride.

The courage of our army reminds us that true heroes wear uniforms, not capes.

Inspirational Army Quotations:

"The soldier is the Army. No army is better than its soldiers." – General George S. Patton
"Service before self." – Indian Army Motto
"Our flag does not fly because the wind moves it; it flies with the last breath of each soldier who died protecting it."
